Q&A

Question: What is going to happen to my data? Will anything be deleted?

Most likely no. If anything gets deleted, it will most likely be just anything from the past day because we take backups every day. It's very unlikely anything will get deleted and we will not be able to recover it.

Question: Is an attacker stealing the database or something?

No, if you saw the temporarily unavailable message, absolutely nothing including ourselves can access the servers from the public internet. Even if an attacker had access to our internal server panel, we would most likely transfer our entire database securely to a different server and delete the one on SocialTap until the threat no longer exists.

Question: What if a hacker was so good that they bypassed all the security methods and got the database file?

Everything except usernames, general unimportant user data (only badges and last online dates) and some unencrypted messages would be exposed. Emails have a zero percent chance of being unencrypted, and passwords are as secure as for example how long you made them and how many random characters there are.
